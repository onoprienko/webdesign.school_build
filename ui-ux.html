What will I learn in the course?

Introduction

Курс состоит из 5 занятий, общей длительностью 1:40

01.
Introduction to Figma

We are going to start with Figma toolbox: interface, main tools, masks, styles. Everything you need to create a website design.

02.
Composition

In this lesson, we will figure out how to create great compositions and how they work. We are going to learn various composition techniques and useful tips to get your composition right.

03.
Colors

In this lesson, we will examine the nature of color. We will talk about what color schemes are and how to choose matching colours.

Let's take a look at successful color combinations and see why their use is preferable.

04.
Typography

We will examine in detail different fonts, which ones are better for web design and how to use them. We’ll also take a look at the main trends in typography.

We will explore such concepts as kerning, tracking,
size, ligature, grapheme, leading and many more.

05.
Grids

In this lesson we will learn how to work with grids in web design, what the pitfalls are and how to create your own solid grid system.

06.
Trends

We will discuss the main trends in web design: what is popular today, what is going to become the new black in the future, and the hypes that shouldn’t mislead you.

07.
Workflow

In this lesson we will analyze each stage of website creation. You will have a ready-to-use workflow that you can use right off in the future.

08.
Types of Website and Terminology

You will discover what types of sites exist and what the difference between a landing and an online store is(if there is any). Also, you’ll get to know what details are worth paying attention to.

Besides that, we will also focus on the terminology and the anatomy of websites.

09.
UX-Designing

In this lesson we will learn how to design interfaces that people will love for their convenience and user-friendliness. We will analyze all the stages of the design process.

10.
Concept

We will discuss how to think through and create a good website concept that will be easy to follow from the first to the last page.

11.
Good Website Rules

We are going to learn how to maintain the integrity of a website using styles, colors and well-chosen graphics and icons.

12.
Emotional Design

How to evoke users’ emotions? Create your own promo for the site that will leave your audience captivated.

13.
Main Page

In this lesson, we will construct a unique design for a home page with precise comments on each decision made.

14.
Inside Pages

We will continue the project creating 1-2 internal pages matching the style of the main page. We’ll discuss possible stumbling blocks and difficulties of page design.

15.
Mobile Version

We are going to create a mobile version of a home page. You’ll learn the rules of adapting design to mobile devices.

16.
UI Kit

We will configure all the styles, prepare the
project for further development and create a real UI -kit.

17.
Portfolio Insights

You will get to know how to create your own portfolio and what the best ways of promoting yourself are. Also we will talk about the most relevant platforms and share our own professional insights with you.

18.
Landing a Job

We will discuss in detail how your new skills can be applied after the course, where you can look for work and what is the best way to get the job you want. Moreover, we’ll explore the advantages and disadvantages of office work and freelance.
